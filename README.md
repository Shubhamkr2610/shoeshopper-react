### In Home

- redirect to product-page , click on shop now button
- click on the Category in which you want to buy

### In Products Page

  - filter the products based on your preference. Shoeshoppers gives filter options for

         filter by Category
         filter by Brand
         Sort by Price
         filter by Ratings

- add Items in your Cart
